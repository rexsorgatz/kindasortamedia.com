Premiere Date
This is manually entered.
Networks
Networks are manually added, as are the links to the pages on their sites.
Recurrence
The time recurrence is added manually.
Metadata
The genre tags and ratings are pulled in from gracenote. The networks are manually entered. The year is from gracenote, but can be manually overwritten.
Description
This is often manually entered at first, and then a better gracenote one eventually comes in. Sometimes that is edited a little bit to best convey the show.
Lists
These are editorial content and they appear automatically.
Episodes
This comes in via gracenote.
TV Schedule
This comes in via gracenote.
Streaming
The streaming sources come in from JustWatch. The IDs are manually entered.
Cast & Crew
This comes from gracenote.
News
This is editorial content for the page.
Review
This is editorial content that comes in automatically.
Key Art
Key art usually starts off coming from TVI editorial. As the show gets closer to premiere, it gets swapped out for Gracenote images.
YouTube
The YouTube links are manually entered and the label (type/season) is also added then.
RottenTomatoes
The RottenTomatoes IDs are manually entered and the scores are updated on-demand.
Awards
Awards data comes from Gracenote.
IMDB
The IMDB ID is manually entered. The IMDB Score gets pulled in via the Open Movie Database.
